# ETR Employee Policy Refresher — June 2025

Open `index.html` in Microsoft Edge or Google Chrome.

## Course flow
1. Open a policy module.
2. Review the key points and detailed policy wording, or use the built-in narration.
3. Complete the knowledge check at the end of the module.
4. Acknowledge the policy and complete the module.
5. After all 31 modules are complete, the final exam unlocks.
6. Score 80% or higher on the final exam to unlock the printable certificate.

Progress, module-check results and exam score are stored locally in the browser.
